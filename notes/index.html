Notes

From time-to-time I may post notes on this site. The primary motivation for this is to intise critical feedback and thereby advance my understanding of certain ideas. I take inspiration here from Andy Matuschak ‘working with his garage door up’. I dont anticipate a readership per se. However, all and any critique or commentry I happen to get will be welcome food for thought and possibly even a chance to meet others chewing on similar ideas or themes.

While continuing to post these types of notes, book reviews etc to other sites and apps would likely be equally rewarding, I’ve become increasingly aware of the value in the Indieweb practice of Publish (on your) Own Site, Syndicate Elsewhere (POSSE). The potential for POSSE to reduce third-party dependencies and extending one’s reach into multiple walled gardens are particularly attractive. New technologies like the proposed W3C Webmention standard and the Fediverse also look to be making POSSE implementation easier than in the past.
